Introduction

Monetizing and creating subscriptions for your Discord server & Discord bots can be a fantastic side-hustle if you are looking to increase your income while putting in minimal effort. There is no downside to monetizing your Discord server, with Discord promoting monetization of servers on its platform. This guide will give you an overview of the ways you can monetize your server and the best software available to do so. This guide will cover the following:

  1. Prerequisites before starting your subscription
  2. Choosing software to monetize with
  3. Reasons why Whop is the best monetization option
  4. Creating your Whop account
  5. Creating your product page
  6. Exploring Whops features and customization options

Prerequisites before starting your subscription business

Before starting your discord-based subscription empire. It is important that you have a product worth selling. This could either mean setting up your discord server or making minor adjustments to ensure your server or bot, is ready to go to market.

If you are yet to fully set up your discord server, having issues with creating discord roles or need further assistance. We would suggest you check out our other discord guides.

Creating your Whop account

Now that you have decided that Whop is the best choice for monetizing your discord server and/or bots. This guide will breakdown how you can set up your Whop account and start monetizing.

The first step is to set up your Whop account, this is first step in starting your business and is where you will manage all of your operations from.

Simply head over to Whop.com and make an account by using the button in the top right corner.

Creating your product page

Now that you have finalized your Whop account, you will need to create a product page on Whop. Your product page is where you will send customers so that they can learn about your company and buy your service / access to your discord server.

To do this, simply click the 'Sell' button in the top right-hand corner, or navigate directly to our business site here.

Once you have done so, you will need to follow the onboarding steps which go as follows:

  1. Create a new community
  2. Add a company name & your twitter handle (optional). (Adding twitter will automatically add your profile picture, bio and link on your product page.)
  3. Select the category that best describes your business or bot
  4. Choose your pricing. (You can make your servers free to join, require an NFT or just have fixed currency amount.)
  5. Input a slogan and a logo (Or your twitter profile picture)
Pro Tip: Make sure to complete the Checklist to be eligible to Launch your Community on Whop and start Earning.
